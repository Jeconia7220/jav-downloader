Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2024

Privacy First: JAV Downloader Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how JAV Downloader Pro ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our video downloading service ("Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use our Service, we may collect the following information:

  • YouTube URLs: The URLs you provide to download videos (processed in real-time, not stored)
  • Format Preferences: Your selected download format and quality settings (stored locally in your browser)
  • Browser Settings: Theme preferences and other UI settings (stored locally in your browser via localStorage)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our Service:

Data Type Purpose Retention Period
IP Addresses Rate limiting, abuse prevention, and security 7-30 days in logs
Request Timestamps Rate limiting and service optimization Temporary (in-memory)
User Agent Strings Browser compatibility and troubleshooting 7-30 days in logs
Error Logs Troubleshooting and service improvement 30-90 days

2.3 Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage (not cookies) to store:

  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Download history (stored only on your device)
  • UI preferences

Important: All this data is stored locally in your browser and is never transmitted to our servers. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings or our "Clear Data" feature.

3. What We DO NOT Collect or Store

Privacy Guarantee: We prioritize your privacy by NOT collecting or storing:
  • ✅ Downloaded Video Files: We do NOT store any video or audio files you download on our servers
  • ✅ Video Content: We do NOT cache, store, or retain any video content from YouTube
  • ✅ Personal Information: We do NOT require registration, accounts, or personal details
  • ✅ Email Addresses: We do NOT collect email addresses (no account required)
  • ✅ Names or Identities: We do NOT ask for or store your name or identity
  • ✅ Payment Information: We do NOT process payments or store financial data (service is free)
  • ✅ Browsing History: We do NOT track your browsing history outside of our Service
  • ✅ Download History: Download history is stored ONLY in your browser's localStorage, never on our servers

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect for the following purposes:

4.1 Service Delivery

  • Processing your download requests in real-time
  • Providing video information and metadata
  • Delivering the requested files to your device

4.2 Security and Abuse Prevention

  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse and ensure fair usage
  • Detecting and blocking malicious activity
  • Preventing automated scraping or bot activity
  • Protecting against DDoS attacks

4.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyzing error logs to fix bugs and improve reliability
  • Understanding usage patterns to optimize performance
  • Troubleshooting technical issues

5. Data Storage and Processing

5.1 Where Your Data is Processed

When you use our Service:

  • Video Downloads: Processed directly from YouTube's servers to your device (we act as a intermediary)
  • Temporary Processing: Video URLs and metadata are processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • Server Location: Our servers may be located in [Your Server Location/Region]

5.2 Data Flow

  1. You provide a YouTube URL to our Service
  2. Our server processes the URL and fetches metadata from YouTube
  3. You select download format and quality
  4. Video content is streamed directly from YouTube's servers to your device through our Service
  5. The download completes and is saved to your device
  6. No video files are retained on our servers

8. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

8.1 Data Control

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Since we don't store personal data long-term, there's minimal data to access
  • Deletion: You can clear your browser's localStorage at any time to remove download history and preferences
  • Opt-Out: You can stop using the Service at any time
  • Clear History: Use our "Clear Data" feature or your browser's settings to remove all locally stored data

8.2 How to Clear Your Data

Method 1 - Using Our Service:

  • Click the "Clear All Data" link in the footer
  • Confirm deletion
  • All local data will be removed

Method 2 - Browser Settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data → Select "Cookies and other site data" and "Cached images and files"
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data → Remove All
